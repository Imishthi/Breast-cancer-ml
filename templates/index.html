What is Predict?

Predict is an AI-powered tool that analyzes medical data or breast images to assess the risk of breast cancer. It uses machine learning models to deliver quick and accurate predictions, supporting early detection and better health decisions.

What is Detect?

Detect is a companion tool designed to help identify early signs of breast cancer using advanced pattern recognition and machine learning techniques. It aids in risk assessment based on patient imaging and biomarker data.

Detect supports early intervention planning and improves diagnostic confidence.

What is Image Classifier?

This feature allows users to upload a medical image (e.g., a scan or biopsy image) and uses an advanced deep learning model (CNN) to automatically classify whether it shows signs of breast cancer.

It's a fast, AI-powered tool that aids in early detection and screening.

What does Predict/Detect do?

Predict/Detectasks for some details about the patient and the cancer. It then uses data about the survival of similar women in the past to show the likely proportion of such women expected to survive up to fifteen years after their surgery with different treatment combinations.

Who is Predict/Detect for?

Predict/Detect is for clinicians, patients and their families. Patients should use it in consultation with a medical professional.
