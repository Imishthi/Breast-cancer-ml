Breast Cancer Treatment Options

Treatment Overview: Most treatment plans include a combination of surgery, radiation, hormone therapy, chemotherapy, and targeted therapies.
Choosing Your Doctor: Your care team may include a medical oncologist, surgical oncologist, and other specialists.
Chemotherapy: Uses drugs to destroy or slow cancer cell growth.
Radiation Therapy: Uses high-energy rays to kill remaining cancer cells after surgery.
Hormone Therapy: Blocks or lowers hormones to treat hormone receptor-positive breast cancers.
Targeted Therapy: Uses drugs to block the growth of cancer cells without harming normal cells.
Managing Side Effects: Many side effects can be managed with your care team’s help.
Standard vs. Clinical Trials: Standard treatments are proven; trials may offer new options.
Wellness & Nutrition: Healthy habits during treatment support overall well-being.
Follow-Up Care: Regular checkups help monitor your health after treatment.
Surgery: Common procedures include lumpectomy, mastectomy, and reconstruction.
