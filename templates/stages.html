Stages of Breast Cancer

Stage 0 Breast Cancer Overview: Stage 0 is the earliest stage of breast cancer. It is non-invasive and has not spread. Stage 0 breast cancer is highly treatable.
Stage 1 Breast Cancer Overview: Stage 1 is the earliest stage of invasive breast cancer, where the cancer has begun to spread into surrounding tissue or lymph nodes.
Stage 2 Breast Cancer Overview: Stage 2 is invasive breast cancer that has grown and spread but is still contained within the breast tissue or nearby lymph nodes.
Stage 3 Breast Cancer Overview: Stage 3 is locally advanced, invasive breast cancer that has spread out of the original area and into lymph nodes or muscle close to the breast.
Stage 4 Breast Cancer Overview: Stage 4 is advanced, metastatic breast cancer that has spread from the breast to other areas of the body, such as the bones, lungs, liver, or brain.
